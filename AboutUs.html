Hello, on this site, I publish my blogs where I photograph my travels, which is my favorite hobby, and take notes about it.

It is important to travel and what you do while traveling before writing. In my father's words, I am walking around with my sense organs open. I lift my head from my phone and look around. I'm discovering what's left and right. I'm lost in the streets Whenever I get lost in a city, I discover great shops and restaurants in that city. If a good smell hits my nose, I always follow it. I trust the waiter of the restaurant I go to and let him choose the food I will eat. I talk to people in the bar I sit in, in the store I enter or while waiting for a taxi, I ask questions.

I wish I could keep all these savings in my mind, but after a while I can forget some things. That's why I always carry a notebook with me. Before I go anywhere, I write the recommendations of my friends and the restaurants, places, shops and museums that I am curious about as a result of my research. While visiting these places, I take small notes for convenience while writing a blog.
